Understanding Hawaiian Electric’s Core Business

Hawaiian Electric Industries serves as the primary electricity provider for the Hawaiian Islands, a natural monopoly with limited direct competition. The company manages power generation, transmission, and distribution while navigating Hawaii’s ambitious clean energy goals. These goals include transitioning to 100% renewable energy by 2045 — a transformative challenge that requires significant capital investments and regulatory collaboration.

This monopoly status delivers predictable cash flow streams, often favored by income investors, but also places HE under regulatory scrutiny to balance affordability and sustainability.
