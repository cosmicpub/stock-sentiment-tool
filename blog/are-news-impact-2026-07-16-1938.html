Understanding ARE’s Business Model

Alexandria RE focuses on owning and operating innovative campuses primarily leased to life science and technology firms. This specialization provides a hedge distinct from traditional commercial real estate, benefiting from growing demand tied to biotech and pharmaceutical research.

The company’s deep-rooted relationships with tenants in high-growth tech hubs like Boston and San Francisco create long-term lease stability, which has historically delivered steady cash flows and attractive dividend yields.
