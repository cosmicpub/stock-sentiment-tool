Understanding Saratoga Investment Corp’s Business Model

Saratoga Investment Corp operates as a BDC, which means it focuses on providing debt and equity financing to private, lower-middle market companies that often have limited access to traditional bank loans or public capital markets. The company earns interest income and capital gains through a diversified portfolio that blends senior loans, subordinated debt, and equity stakes.

This model aims to offer investors attractive dividend income, typically with distributions paid quarterly. The effectiveness of SAY’s strategy depends strongly on credit quality and economic conditions impacting its portfolio companies.
