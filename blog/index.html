How to Use These Reports

Stock sentiment can be useful, but it should be understood the right way.

Sentiment is not a price guarantee

A bullish or bearish reading reflects current headline pressure, not a sure future move in the stock.

Context matters

A single headline may sound positive or negative, but the broader mix of legal, macro, earnings, product, or industry news often tells the real story.

Use sentiment with other signals

Sentiment works best alongside price action, valuation, earnings quality, and overall market conditions.
