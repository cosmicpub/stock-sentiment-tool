Understanding Two Harbors' Business Model

Two Harbors is a mortgage real estate investment trust specializing in agency and non-agency residential mortgage-backed securities (RMBS). Its earnings hinge on effectively managing the spread between borrowing costs and the yields on mortgage assets. This structure means TWO is highly sensitive to interest rate fluctuations and prepayment risks.

Why the Interest Rate Environment Matters
