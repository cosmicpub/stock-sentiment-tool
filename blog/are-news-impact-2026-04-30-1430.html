Why ARE’s Niche Is Important

This targeted approach gives ARE a moat in competitive innovation hubs such as Boston and San Francisco, where demand for specialized lab and office space remains robust.

The rise of biotech innovation and an increased emphasis on R&D spending has created sustained demand for such assets, insulating ARE from typical retail or residential real estate cyclicality.
