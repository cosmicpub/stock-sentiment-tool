Growth Catalysts Behind ON Semiconductor’s Momentum

Moreover, ON’s recent acquisitions have expanded its portfolio, enabling cross-selling opportunities in energy-efficient solutions and industrial IoT devices. This strategic pivot benefits from macro trends such as renewable energy adoption and smart infrastructure upgrades.
