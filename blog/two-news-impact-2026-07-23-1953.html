Understanding TWO’s Market Position and Business Model

Two Harbors focuses on investing in residential mortgage-backed securities, primarily agency RMBS, alongside other real estate-related investments. The mortgage REIT model generates income through the interest spread—the difference between borrowing costs and the yield on mortgage assets.

This spread can fluctuate with interest rate movements and prepayment speeds, which are highly sensitive to economic factors and Federal Reserve policy. TWO's ability to manage duration risk and hedge interest exposure is key to maintaining consistent dividends for shareholders.
