Understanding Alexandria Real Estate Equities’ Business Model

This niche focus gives ARE an advantage: strong, long-term lease contracts with high-credit tenants paired with steady demand driven by ongoing R&D investments. Given the continued growth in biotech innovation pipelines, this focus is a differentiator among REITs and could translate into relatively stable cash flows.
