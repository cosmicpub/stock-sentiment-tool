Industry and Geopolitical Challenges

The apparel sector remains vulnerable due to ongoing geopolitical conflicts, notably within the Middle East region. As shown recently with Abercrombie & Fitch, European, Middle Eastern, and African sales are declining sharply—some by as much as 10%—driven by unrest and supply chain disruptions. Amer Sports likely faces similar regional pressures, contributing to investor wariness.

Furthermore, broader economic factors such as fluctuating commodity prices and changing consumer spending habits in luxury goods could dampen revenue growth prospects. A reported drop in U.S. oil prices points to shifting energy markets, which indirectly affect textile and apparel production costs and logistics.

Competitive Position and Market Dynamics
