Understanding Two Harbors’ Business Model

Two Harbors Investment Corp is primarily a mortgage real estate investment trust (mREIT) focusing on agency mortgage-backed securities (MBS). It capitalizes on spreads between borrowing costs and yields on mortgage assets, deriving income largely from interest rate arbitrage. This model tends to thrive in stable or declining interest rate environments but faces pressure when rates rise or housing market volatility spikes.
