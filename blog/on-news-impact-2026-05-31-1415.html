Business Fundamentals and Growth Drivers

ON Semiconductor has carved out a strong niche in power efficient analog semiconductors, gaining traction in automotive applications such as electric and autonomous vehicles, as well as industrial automation—both key growth markets. Their investments in advanced packaging and silicon carbide technology position ON well to capture rising demand for higher-powered solutions.

Additionally, the shift toward decentralization of semiconductor manufacturing may benefit ON by keeping production closer to key automotive and factory customers, giving them supply chain resilience advantages.

Competitive Landscape and Market Position
