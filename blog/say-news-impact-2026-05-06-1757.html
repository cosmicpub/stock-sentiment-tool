Understanding Saratoga Investment Corp’s Business Model

Saratoga operates primarily as a BDC, providing tailored financing solutions to private companies. Unlike traditional banks, BDCs often take larger, direct stakes in borrower companies, yielding higher returns but also carrying elevated credit risk. Saratoga’s strategy emphasizes rigorous underwriting and portfolio diversification across sectors and geographies to mitigate risk.

Investors receive income mainly through quarterly dividends sourced from interest payments on these loans, making SAY attractive to income-focused portfolios, especially in the low-yield environment.
