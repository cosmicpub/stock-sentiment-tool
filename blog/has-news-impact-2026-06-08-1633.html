Leisure Products Industry Outlook

The leisure products sector has been steady but faces disruptive forces such as increasing digital content consumption and emerging competitive startups. While traditional toy sales fluctuate, the segment’s pivot toward hybrid physical-digital products opens new avenues for growth.

Post-pandemic consumer patterns reveal a rebound in discretionary spending on family and children's entertainment, bolstering Hasbro’s market position. However, supply chain constraints and raw material price volatility remain ongoing risks for manufacturers.
