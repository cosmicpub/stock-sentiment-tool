Understanding Two Harbors Investment's Business Model

Two Harbors primarily invests in residential mortgage-backed securities (RMBS) and real estate-related assets. As an mREIT, it earns income by borrowing at short-term rates and investing in longer-duration mortgage assets, capturing the spread. This inherently exposes it to interest rate risk and credit risk, which must be managed carefully to protect shareholder value.
