Understanding Two Harbors’ Business Model

Two Harbors specializes in investing in residential mortgage-backed securities (RMBS), agency and non-agency mortgage loans, and related assets. The company’s income largely derives from the interest payments on these assets, making it sensitive to interest rate changes and prepayment risks. Investors looking at TWO are essentially betting on the company’s ability to manage interest rate shifts while maintaining attractive dividend yields.
