Industry Dynamics Supporting ON Semiconductor's Growth

The semiconductor sector is riding a wave of demand stemming from automotive electrification, 5G rollouts, AI integration, and smart device proliferation. ON Semiconductor, with its diverse product portfolio targeting power management, automotive sensors, and industrial applications, is positioned to capitalize on these trends.

  • Industrial and IoT Markets: Rising automation in industrial sectors and IoT device proliferation offer steady demand for ON's mixed-signal and analog semiconductors.
