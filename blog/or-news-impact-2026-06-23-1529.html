Understanding OR’s Business Model

OR Royalties Inc specializes in acquiring metal royalties and streams, providing it non-operational revenue exposure to mining outputs rather than direct ore extraction. This model cushions the company from typical operational risks like labor disputes or production delays, allowing more consistent cash flow streams tied to commodity sales.

This royalty approach can be appealing during volatile cycles in mining as OR benefits when metal prices rise but is somewhat insulated when operations face challenges. Investors often view this as a lower-risk route into metals & mining sector gains.
