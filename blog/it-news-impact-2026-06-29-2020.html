Why Is Gartner Stock Moving Bearish?

According to CNBC, "Everything tied to the data center is suddenly suspect," reflecting investor caution as hyperscalers grapple with AI-driven capacity and cost pressures. This pervasive uncertainty has dampened tech enthusiasm, dragging Gartner's stock down despite its less capital-intensive model.

Additionally, global geopolitical risks, such as tensions in the Middle East, add layers of macroeconomic uncertainty that indirectly impact technology investments across sectors.
