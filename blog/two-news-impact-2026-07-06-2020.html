Understanding Two Harbors’ Business Model

Two Harbors specializes in investing primarily in residential mortgage-backed securities (RMBS), generating income through the spread between the interest earned on assets and the cost of financing. This business model hinges heavily on interest rate movements and the prepayment behavior of underlying mortgages.

The firm has been refining its portfolio to balance credit risk and interest rate exposure, seeking to deliver sustainable dividends. For investors, this means TWO offers a unique blend of steady income paired with notable market sensitivity.
