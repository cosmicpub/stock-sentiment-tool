Industry Position and Business Overview

HE provides electricity and energy services predominantly across the Hawaiian Islands. The utility faces unique challenges due to geographic isolation, which limits fuel sourcing options and necessitates strong focus on renewable integration. The company is advancing efforts to increase reliance on clean energy sources in line with Hawaii's aggressive clean energy mandates targeting 100% renewable energy by 2045.

This transition aligns HE with global sustainability trends but involves high capital expenditures and operational complexities. Securing regulatory support for infrastructure investments remains a pivotal factor in future profitability.
