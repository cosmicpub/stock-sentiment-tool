Key Growth Drivers for ON Semiconductor

One of ON Semiconductor's primary growth engines is the expanding ecosystem around artificial intelligence and electric vehicles. The company’s specialization in power and sensing solutions positions it well in markets such as autonomous vehicles, industrial automation, and 5G infrastructure.

Moreover, semiconductor demand surges linked to AI infrastructure are creating a "networking supercycle," as underscored by peers like Cisco. This trend translates to stronger orders and revenue potential for ON’s product lines.

Finally, their automotive segment's robust order backlog benefits from the ongoing electrification and safety features push. This diversified demand base reduces reliance on any single industry segment, adding resilience.
