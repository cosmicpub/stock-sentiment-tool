Understanding OR Royalties’ Business Model

OR Royalties is a royalty and streaming company, meaning it earns revenue by receiving a portion of the production or revenue from mining operations without having to operate mines directly. This model offers lower operational risk compared to miners, but it does tie the company’s earnings closely to metal prices and mining output.

The metals and mining industry is cyclical, and OR benefits when commodity prices are rising, as royalties become more valuable and streaming volumes increase. However, during prolonged downturns or supply constraints, revenue may stagnate or fall, impacting the stock negatively.
