The leisure products sector has undergone significant evolution, with digital entertainment increasingly capturing consumer attention. Hasbro’s legacy strength in board games, action figures, and traditional toys confronts rising competition from both digital-first companies and fast-moving consumer trends. However, Hasbro continues to leverage intellectual property like Transformers and Monopoly to maintain relevance.
