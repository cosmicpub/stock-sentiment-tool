What happened today

Shares of Gartner Inc (ticker: IT) closed at $154.88, down by $0.65 or approximately 0.42% on April 17, 2026. The stock experienced a slight decline despite a broadly bullish sentiment rating of 2 with high confidence, indicating investor optimism about the company's prospects.

On the news front, CNBC featured Jim Cramer highlighting that investors will soon "gravitate" back to this megacap stock, which is reflective of renewed interest in large technology companies such as Gartner. Concurrently, global markets rallied significantly due to geopolitical developments regarding Iran's announcement that the Strait of Hormuz is "completely open," signaling a reduction in regional tension.

Reuters reported multiple updates around Iran's statements on the Strait of Hormuz remaining open during a ceasefire, leading to surging global markets. This geo-political easing also catalyzed hedge fund buying worth $86 billion, according to Goldman data cited by Reuters. Meanwhile, European oil majors outperformed their U.S. counterparts, capitalizing on trading opportunities amid the Iran conflict context. The technology sector, where Gartner operates, sits amid these macroeconomic dynamics.
